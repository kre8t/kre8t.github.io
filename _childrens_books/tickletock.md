Dive into the magical world of the tide pools!
The Tickle-Tock Tide Pool takes young readers on a whimsical, rhyming adventure beneath the waves, where colorful sea creatures come to life in the style of Dr. Seuss.

Meet Sammy the Sea Star, a crab with a sideways strut, a shy sea anemone, and a giggling shrimp who keeps the pool sparkling clean! Each page is filled with playful rhythm and curious wonder that will delight kids and parents alike.

Perfect for children ages 3–8, this beautifully written story teaches about marine life and the amazing little worlds that exist where the ocean meets the shore. With its joyful rhymes, lively imagination, and love for nature, The Tickle-Tock Tide Pool inspires curiosity, kindness, and care for our oceans.

Whether read aloud at bedtime or explored on a beach-day adventure, this book will have your little one saying,
“Let’s go see the tide pools!”
