Dive into an undersea adventure filled with rhythm, friendship, and the joy of learning!

When Otto the Octopus discovers a mysterious guitar washed up near his reef, he dreams of becoming the greatest musician in the ocean. There’s just one problem - he’s terrible at it! But Otto doesn’t give up. With practice, patience, and plenty of determination, he learns to play, starts a band with his sea-creature friends, and soon fills the ocean with music and laughter.

This heartwarming rhyming story celebrates creativity, perseverance, and the magic of making music together. From jellyfish and dolphins to crabs on percussion, kids will love meeting Otto's colorful ocean band and discovering how one octopus’s dream led to the very first “school of fish!”
